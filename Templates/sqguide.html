Squat


The squat is a dynamic strength training exercise that requires several muscles in the upper and lower body to work together simultaneously. The squat is one of the most effective, trusted source exercises for enhancing athletic performance. It is relatively easy for most people to perform because it does not require any equipment. There are contain few types of squats. Wall squats, Box squats, Squat jumps, Goblet squats few of them.
