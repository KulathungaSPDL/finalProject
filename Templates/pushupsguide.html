Push Ups


Push-ups are a convenient exercise that can do anywhere. They do not need equipment or a gym membership. This exercise can help users torch calories, build and tone muscle, gain strength, and positively impact their overall fitness, function, and performance. Most people think of push-ups as a strength exercise, Push-ups can also improve heart health.







Watch on youtube




Instruction



Before starting Push ups users sit in a quadruped position and hands directly underneath with shoulders and knees directly underneath with hips.


Engage body core and extend one leg out then bring the other to meet it forming one nice long position. Focus on keeping a nice neutral spine throughout with focus directly in between hands. Feet should be hip-width apart and parallel to each other, not turned inward or outward


When the user inhales, lower body down to hover off of the ground, forming a 90-degree angle from shoulder, elbow to hand. and when exhaling, push the floor away keeping the body strong and tight.
